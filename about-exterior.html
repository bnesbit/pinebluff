Incredible Views

Pine Bluff Retreat is situated at the end of a quiet peninsula with lake views on three sides, where you will always feel immersed in the peaceful serenity of nature.

Privacy

There is only one other cottage at the end of this peninsula. Can you see it? It's only readily visible when the foliage has left the trees in the fall.

The Iron Wheel Bar & Grill

The Iron Wheel is a large, double fire pit next to a large, durable picnic table that is perfect for keeping warm, toasting marshmallows and having laughs. Built to entertain.

The Dock

Well built concrete steps lead down to the boat dock, where you can tie up your boat or make use of the small watercraft that are available, including an aluminum boat with trolling motor (or oars), paddle boat or canoe. Also a great spot for casting a line or sitting back and enjoying nature at it's best.

Camping Area & Boat Launch

There is plenty of room for tents and campers, including on the lawn behind the Iron Wheel. Also located on the property is a small boat launch that is perfect for small boats.

Lots of Parking

There is plenty of room for vehicles, if you can avoid the turkeys out there.
